## Getting the habit of programming daily

I started learning to program after finishing college and during the time my job would let me do it, so I found hard to find time every day to spend in 
front of the computer after hours. Then I was learning lots of stuff that eventually I forgot or never knew how to apply, due to the lack of practice. 
That's why I purchased the udemy course of 100 days of code, or dr. Yu, to commit myself with the daily practice of coding.

Even I found the first days quite basic and trivial, was interesting to follow the videos and to the challenges, at least to get more used to python's
syntax, specially because I learned to program in Java, and also got some tips I didn't know.
